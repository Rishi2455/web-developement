WELCOME TO GEC BHAVNAGAR

Government Engineering College, Bhavnagar is one of the leading technical institutes in Gujarat. It was established in 2004 and is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines of engineering. The college has a well-qualified faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure to provide quality education to its students. The college also has a placement cell that helps students secure job opportunities in reputed companies. The college is committed to providing a conducive learning environment to its students and preparing them for a successful career in the field of engineering.

VISION

To be a leading technical institute in the country that imparts quality education and produces skilled professionals who contribute to the development of society.

MISSION

To provide quality education in the field of engineering and technology through innovative teaching methods and state-of-the-art infrastructure. To foster a culture of research and innovation among students and faculty. To collaborate with industry and academia to bridge the gap between theory and practice. To instill ethical values and social responsibility in students and prepare them for a successful career in the field of engineering.
